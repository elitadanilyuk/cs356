1. 1 sentence: What goes in the /var directory on a UNIX computer?
	/var contains files to which the system writes data during the course of its operation (variable data).
2. 1 sentence: What is the /dev directory for on a UNIX computer?
	/dev consists of files that represent devices that are attached to the local system (device files).
